The purposeful way to use your computer

Mice and keyboards are outdated, uninteresting, and too complicated.

With a quick Yank!, your computer can:

  • Immediately end video calls
  • Play and pause your music
  • Mute and unmute your mic
  • Play sounds
  • Hit a hotkey
  • ...do anything you can script

Features

  • Wireless Yank! device
  • 1 meter long chain
  • Compatible with macOS with the Yank! Companion App (download here)
  • Chargeable via USB
  • 1 month battery life
  • Mountable on ceiling, wall, or shelf (with clamp)
  • Loss of dignity
